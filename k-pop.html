SEVENTEEN

SEVENTEEN or usually styled as SVT is a K-POP boy band that debuted in May 25th, 2015 ... Although they are called Seventeen, they actually have 13 members. (Seungcheol, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, WOOZI, DK, Mingyu, THE8, Seungkwan, Vernon, Dino). The name SEVENTEEN comes from 13 (members) + 3 (sub-units) + 1 (team) = 17. Those sub-units are Vocal Team, Hip-Hop Team, and Performance Team.
